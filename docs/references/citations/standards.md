# Citation Standards

This textbook follows APA (American Psychological Association) style for citations as specified in the project requirements.

## In-text Citations

- (Author, Year) format for paraphrasing
- (Author, Year, p. #) format for direct quotes
- Multiple authors: (Author1 & Author2, Year) or (Author1 et al., Year) for 3+ authors

## Bibliography Template

Entries should follow APA format:

- Journal article: Author, A. A. (Year). Title of article. Title of Periodical, volume(issue), pages. https://doi.org/xx.xxxx/xxxxx
- Book: Author, B. B. (Year). Title of book. Publisher.
- Conference paper: Author, C. C. (Year). Title of paper. Title of Conference, pages. Publisher.
- Website: Author, D. D. (Year, Month Date). Title of webpage. Title of Website. URL

## Tools for Citation Management

Consider using citation management tools such as:
- Zotero
- Mendeley
- EndNote
