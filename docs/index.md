# Welcome to the AI Robotics Textbook

This textbook covers Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics, providing students with comprehensive knowledge of ROS 2, Digital Twin simulation, NVIDIA Isaac, Vision-Language-Action (VLA) integration, and a Capstone project.

## Modules

- [ROS 2 Fundamentals](./modules/ros2/introduction.md)
- [Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)](./modules/digital-twin/introduction.md)
- [NVIDIA Isaac (AI Perception)](./modules/nvidia-isaac/perception.md)
- [Vision-Language-Action (VLA)](./modules/vla/voice-control.md)
- [Capstone Project](./modules/capstone/project-outline.md)

## Getting Started

1. Start with the [ROS 2 Fundamentals](./modules/ros2/introduction.md) module
2. Progress through each module sequentially
3. Complete the exercises and assessments
4. Finish with the Capstone Project

## About This Textbook

This textbook is designed for advanced high school to early college students with background in computer science, engineering, or robotics.
